Personal Life and Education

I'm Jan Šušteršič, welcome to my webpage. I was born in Maribor, Slovenia in 1992 and I lived there throughout most of my life. After elementary school I completed the II. gimnazija high school and got my University Bachelors Degree in translation of English and German into Slovenian. In 2017 I moved to Ljubljana to pursue my Masters Degree in that same field, but I dropped out due to stress and inadequacy of the programme. In 2021, after a horrid year with Covid-19 I moved to Joensuu finland, after which I started my studies at Karelia UAS to become an Software Engineer where I am now.

Work Life

Throughout my life I have acquired a colourful list of experiences. In my late teens and early 20s I have helped organize private sports events. I also worked at a different sports organization, preparing the hall for sports matches, concerts, theatre. I was a receptionist, an on-ice monitor and much much more, which really only built upon the versatility I was already known for. I also took on a few translation jobs here and there, most notably I translated a cartoon for dubbing purposes. I shortly worked as a door-to-door salesman for a Slovenian telecommunications company, but so far my longest employment was 13 years at the Stand Up Comedy stage of the international festival Festival Lent. There I started off as a stage-hand, but after 2 years I quickly became team leader and immediately after stage manager, where I even worked the summers when I was already living in Finland.

Karelia UAS Extra-Curriculars

While attending studies at Karelia UAS I have and use the opportunity to be more than just a student. I attend guest lectures and events, I became a class VIP* student. All of that activity lead to me joining multiple activities to better the studies and interactions for international students, which resulted in me becoming the vice-chair of the POKA student union international division and also a tutor for the new students.
*VIP (AOC) - välittämisen ilmapiiri (Atmosphere of Caring)

Hobbies

While I have had plenty of hobbies in the past, they can all be sorted into 1 of 2 categories. First category would be games, as I love board games, card games videogames, sports games, you name it. Recently I have been hooked by an interface which randomizes multiple different games, and connects them in a way that you would have to cooperate with others playing the other games so all of you can finish your games. I wish to at some point also wish to help create an interface for a game that is not yet supported but I enjoy. I do not just play games though. I usually play them socially, aka with others or for others, which leads me to the other category, entertainment. As a hobby I have started streaming videogame content over 10 years ago. I did that because I seemingly love to be on stage with my history in the improv theatre and recently starting my very own Stand Up comedy performance.
